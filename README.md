## Content Classification & Scope

The repository is strictly categorized into three distinct types of technical documentation:

### 1. Research Papers & Analytics
* Deep-dive analyses and critical reviews of peer-reviewed scientific papers (IEEE, ACM, IACR).
* Mathematical proofs and verification of cryptographic primitives and protocols.
* Structural decomposition of core cryptographic algorithms.

### 2. Applied Guides & Implementations
* Step-by-step technical guides on configuring, testing, and deploying secure architectures.
* PoC (Proof of Concept) implementation briefs for cryptographic solutions.
* Methodologies for modern vulnerability exploitation and remediation.

### 3. Threat Intelligence & Vulnerability Research
* Detailed breakdowns of newly discovered architectural and software vulnerabilities.
* Threat modeling for high-load environments and low-level communication protocols.
